Circle Drive

Full of character and for sale!

Posted by Curtis Coddington on September 27th, 2017

Local Realtor, Joan Jarvis, is listing this Spanish style 4 bedroom home that's full of charm and located on an over sized lot in an ideal location. The open floor plan boasts original hardwood floors through out. The large living room is made cozy with a gas fireplace. The formal dining room leads to a private garden that is perfect to relax in or for entertaining guests.



The kitchen leaves no want for additional cabinet space as they go from floor to ceiling. The split plan with spacious bedrooms have a mixture of walk in closets and built in features. The utility room could be converted into a 5th bedroom or even a 3rd bathroom.




3 zoned irrigation with timer runs on a separate irrigation meter that waters the entire yard and flower beds effortlessly. The invisible fence installed in the front and back yard make play time fun and safe for your children and pets. This truly unique home has much to offer!
