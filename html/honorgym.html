Honor Gym in Winter Haven

The coolest place to work out

Posted by Curtis Coddington on October 6th, 2017

Gym owner, Wayne Bell, contacted us recently to capture the newest, and by far trendiest, gym in the heart of Winter Haven. He found us on Google's Trusted Photographer platform and we got right to work.

This freshly renovated building on 3rd Street rocks concrete floors and exposed wood beams on the ceiling. Honor Gym is a family operated fitness center that even caters to the little ones with a playroom.

Take a look at the diverse fitness equipment that will meet any rigorous training needs you may have. Venture into the calisthenics area or get your sweat on in one of their intense classes. Take a look at the tour below and be sure to contact Wayne with any questions.
