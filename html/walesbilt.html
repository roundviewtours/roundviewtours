A bird's eye view of Lake Wales

The Walesbilt Hotel in Lake Wales, Florida.

Posted by Curtis Coddington on October 31st, 2017

We got a sneak peak of the massive renovations that a small and dedicated team are meticulously completing on the Walesbilt Hotel. This local and long forgotten gem was built in 1926 after a stock-sale campaign. The hotel was quite popular in it's time, but until recently has been collecting dust.


That is, until Ray Brown came to town.

a view from atop The Walesbilt Hotel

Mr. Brown gave us the opportunity to trek up the ten flights until we reached the door to the best view in town.


Step out on the rooftop of The Walesbilt Hotel and you are surrounded by all the home town charm, sparkling lakes, and even the iconic view of Bok Tower that Lake Wales is known for.
